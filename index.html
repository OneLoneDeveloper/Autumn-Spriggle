About Autumn

I am a passionate artist who finds inspiration in the gentle transition between seasons and the eternal dance of waves upon the shore. My work captures the fleeting moments where autumn's golden embrace meets the infinite blue of coastal waters.

Through layered textures and glowing color palettes, I aim to evoke calm, wonder, and that quiet feeling of standing at the edge of the world—just before night takes the sky.
